Our Journey

Decades of Innovation and Customer-Centric Logistics

From Trucking Roots to 360° Logistics

Founded in the mid-1990s, GSC began as a regional trucking operation with hundreds of trucks and multiple warehouses, leveraging decades of logistics expertise. Over time, customer needs expanded beyond transportation to 3PL and fulfillment solutions. In response, we streamlined our trucking services and evolved into GSC 360°, providing warehousing, fulfillment, freight management, and specialized handling—all under one roof. Over the years, we've partnered with industry leaders such as Electrolux, Bosch, QVC, Amazon, Mitsubishi, Keurig, Ajinomoto, ASMO, and Saudi German Company.

Our Mission

At GSC 360°, we help businesses large and small "go further" by delivering efficient, reliable, and transparent logistics solutions. We take pride in offering competitive pricing, continuous improvement, and fostering long-term partnerships with our clients.

Our Facilities

With locations in Eastern North Carolina and Virginia, GSC 360° operates three warehouses with a total footprint of 1,000,000 sq. ft., including 158,750 sq. ft. currently available for flexible storage. Climate-controlled storage is available upon request. Our facilities are strategically located near major shipping ports, highways, and one of the largest runways on the East Coast, ensuring seamless logistics. Conveniently situated near Norfolk, Raleigh, Charlotte, Greensboro, Wilmington, and Richmond, we offer rural-level pricing with access to major metropolitan markets—delivering fast and cost-effective solutions for your supply chain needs
