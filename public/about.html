Our Story

A Legacy of Innovation & Customer-Centric Service

From Trucking Beginnings to Full-Service Warehousing

Founded in the mid-1990s as a local trucking company, GSC Logistics quickly discovered a pressing need for flexible, reliable warehousing solutions. Over the years, we transitioned from primarily trucking to a modern warehousing powerhouse—offering storage, packing, fulfillment, and more. Although local trucking remains available on request, our passion today is delivering the best in warehouse management and customer care.

Our Facilities

We operate three spacious warehouses, each carefully chosen for its proximity to major shipping routes, ports, and highways. Whether you’re a small startup looking for overflow storage or a large enterprise seeking a full-scale distribution hub, we have room to grow with you.

Our Team & Values

At GSC, our team is a blend of seasoned logistics pros and enthusiastic innovators. We believe in transparency, reliability, and going the extra mile to ensure our clients’ success. Our mission is simple: make warehousing easy, efficient, and surprisingly delightful.
